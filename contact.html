Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. Don't see yours? Contact us directly.

How quickly can I get started with CareLoop?

Most families can set up their CareLoop account and start coordinating care within 10-15 minutes. Our onboarding process guides you through adding family members, medications, and healthcare providers step by step.

Is my family's health information secure?

Absolutely. CareLoop is HIPAA compliant and uses bank-level encryption to protect all health information. You control exactly what information is shared with family members and healthcare providers.

Do you integrate with my doctor's office?

We integrate with over 50 major healthcare systems and are continuously adding new providers. If your doctor's office uses Epic, Cerner, or Allscripts, integration is usually available. Contact us to check specific providers.

What if my parent isn't comfortable with technology?

CareLoop is designed for all comfort levels with technology. We offer phone-based daily check-ins, large-button interfaces, and family members can manage most coordination tasks remotely. Our support team also provides personalized onboarding assistance.

Can multiple family members use the same account?

Yes! CareLoop is built for family coordination. Multiple adult children can share caregiving responsibilities with different permission levels, and everyone stays informed through shared updates and communications.
