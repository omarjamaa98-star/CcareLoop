Description of Service

CareLoop is an AI-powered eldercare coordination platform designed to help families manage the comprehensive care of aging parents and loved ones. Our Service includes:

  • Medication Management: Tracking, reminders, and coordination with pharmacies
  • Appointment Coordination: Scheduling and managing healthcare appointments
  • Daily Health Check-ins: AI-powered conversations to monitor wellbeing
  • Family Communication: Tools for coordinating care among family members
  • Healthcare Integration: Connections with healthcare providers and systems

CareLoop is a healthcare technology platform and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. We facilitate coordination and communication but do not replace professional medical care.
