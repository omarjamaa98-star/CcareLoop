Introduction

At CareLoop, we understand that your family's health information is deeply personal and sensitive. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share information when you use our eldercare coordination platform.

CareLoop is committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security, including full compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and other applicable privacy laws.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and account preferences
  • Family Information: Names and relationships of family members participating in care coordination
  • Contact Information: Emergency contacts, healthcare provider contact details

Health Information

  • Medical Information: Medication lists, dosage schedules, health conditions, and symptoms
  • Healthcare Provider Data: Appointment schedules, provider notes, and care plans
  • Daily Check-in Data: Responses to health questions, mood tracking, and wellness indicators
  • Emergency Information: Medical alert data and emergency response information

Usage Information

  • Platform Usage: How you interact with CareLoop features and services
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and browser information
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, and pages viewed (anonymized for privacy)

How We Use Your Information

Primary Care Coordination

  • Provide medication reminders and tracking
  • Coordinate appointment scheduling with healthcare providers
  • Facilitate family communication about care needs
  • Generate health summaries and trend reports
  • Provide AI-powered daily check-ins and health monitoring

Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our AI algorithms (using anonymized data)
  • Develop new features based on user needs
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance

Communication

  • Send important service updates and security notifications
  • Provide educational content about eldercare (with your consent)
  • Respond to your questions and support requests

Information Sharing and Disclosure

With Your Consent

We share information only when you explicitly authorize it:

  • Family Members: You control which family members can access specific health information
  • Healthcare Providers: You authorize sharing of relevant health data with your medical team
  • Emergency Contacts: You designate who receives emergency notifications

Service Providers

We work with trusted service providers who help us operate CareLoop:

  • Cloud hosting providers (with HIPAA Business Associate Agreements)
  • Payment processors for subscription billing
  • Customer support platforms
  • Security monitoring services

All service providers are contractually required to protect your information and use it only for authorized purposes.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law:

  • To comply with court orders or legal processes
  • To protect against fraud or security threats
  • In emergency situations to protect health and safety

What We Never Do

  • Sell your personal or health information to third parties
  • Share information with advertisers or marketers
  • Use your health data for purposes unrelated to your care

Data Security

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
  • Access Controls: Multi-factor authentication and role-based access controls
  • Network Security: Firewalls, intrusion detection, and continuous monitoring
  • Regular Audits: Third-party security assessments and penetration testing

Administrative Safeguards

  • Privacy officer oversight and staff training
  • Incident response procedures
  • Regular security policy updates
  • Background checks for all employees with data access

Physical Safeguards

  • Secure data center facilities with controlled access
  • Workstation security requirements
  • Secure disposal of physical media

Your Privacy Rights

Access and Control

  • Access: View all information we have about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format

Sharing Controls

  • Choose which family members can access specific information
  • Control what information is shared with healthcare providers
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Revoke sharing permissions at any time

HIPAA Compliance

Protected Health Information (PHI)

CareLoop is a HIPAA-covered entity, and we treat all health information as Protected Health Information (PHI). This means:

  • We follow strict rules about how PHI is used and shared
  • You have specific rights under HIPAA regarding your health information
  • We maintain detailed records of how your PHI is accessed and used

Business Associate Agreements

All third-party service providers who may access PHI sign HIPAA Business Associate Agreements, ensuring they also protect your health information according to HIPAA standards.

HIPAA Rights

Under HIPAA, you have the right to:

  • Request an accounting of disclosures of your PHI
  • Request restrictions on how your PHI is used or shared
  • File a complaint with the Department of Health and Human Services

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal requirements:

  • Active Accounts: Information is retained while your account is active
  • Closed Accounts: Most data is deleted within 30 days of account closure
  • Legal Requirements: Some information may be retained longer to comply with healthcare regulations
  • Anonymized Data: We may retain anonymized usage data for service improvement

International Users

CareLoop primarily serves users in the United States. If you are located outside the US:

  • Your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States
  • We comply with applicable international privacy laws
  • Contact us for information about specific privacy protections in your region

Children's Privacy

CareLoop is designed for adult caregivers coordinating eldercare. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes:

  • We will notify you by email and through the CareLoop platform
  • Material changes will be highlighted clearly
  • You will have the opportunity to review changes before they take effect
  • Continued use of CareLoop after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy
