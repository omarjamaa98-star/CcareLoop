Transforming Eldercare Through AI and Compassion

CareLoop was founded by caregivers who understand the overwhelming challenge of coordinating care for aging parents. We're building technology that handles the complexity so families can focus on what matters most.

Our Mission

To eliminate the stress and complexity of eldercare coordination through intelligent technology that puts families first.

The Challenge We're Solving

Every day, millions of adult children struggle with the invisible burden of eldercare coordination. Managing medications, scheduling appointments, coordinating with healthcare providers, and ensuring family communication creates overwhelming stress that impacts both caregivers and care recipients.

Traditional solutions are fragmented, requiring families to juggle multiple apps, spreadsheets, and communication channels. Healthcare providers often lack visibility into the full picture of a patient's daily care, leading to missed opportunities for better health outcomes.

Our Solution

CareLoop brings together artificial intelligence, healthcare integration, and thoughtful design to create a comprehensive eldercare coordination platform. We handle the complexity of care coordination so families can focus on spending quality time together and ensuring their loved ones receive the best possible care.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we build and every decision we make.

Empathy First

Every feature is designed with deep understanding of the emotional and practical challenges facing caregiving families. We build with compassion because we've been there.

Privacy & Security

Healthcare data is sacred. We implement HIPAA-compliant security measures and give families complete control over their information sharing preferences.

Accessibility

Technology should work for everyone, regardless of age or technical skill level. We design for accessibility from the ground up, not as an afterthought.

Transparency

Families deserve to understand how our AI makes recommendations and how their data is used. We believe in clear communication and honest relationships.
